PROJECT DESCRIPTION

The Covid19 hurt many employed people including men, women, and specifically The Women JobSeeker whose percentage fell by 35%. We need a solution for these women who are devoted to their work and want to begin earning money and respect by making hustles. The main idea behind this app is to reach every single woman who wants to employ herself in the domain of her passion ‘cooking’. Each woman who is unemployed directly or indirectly seems to face taunting by her family or society. We’re here to provide a platform where she has to register herself with a small number of documents and join as a Tiffin Partner and start selling a tiffin as per her skill. All she has to be added Tiffin Status, Dishes that she’ll include in it, and prices that she wants. Next, The Delivery App will get a notification of Tiffin Provider is ready to provide, and as Any User/Customer will Purchase any tiffin, The Delivery boy will reach to Tiffin Service provider’s Location and Pick it up and Drop it to the location of Customer Safely without any waste of time.
